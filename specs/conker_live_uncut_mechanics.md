# Conker: Live & Uncut – Mechanics Constraints

## Allowed

- N64-style pickups:
  - Weapons placed on map as static items.
  - Health/ammo pickups consistent with original Multi.
- Objective types:
  - CTF-style bag carrying (Heist).
  - Assault/defense lanes (Beach).
  - Zone capture (Total War / Fortress variants).
  - Egg stealing and feeding (Raptor).
  - Canister-style instant-win gas triggers (Tank, Alien Base).
- Movement:
  - Run, crouch, limited jump.
  - Slower movement and no jump when carrying heavy weapons (Bazooka, canisters).

## Forbidden

- Persistent classes (medic, sniper, engineer, etc.).
- XP gain, unlock trees, perks, or meta-progression.
- Loadout customization overriding pickup flow.
- Hit-scan modern assault rifles or gear that breaks N64-era pacing.

If AI tooling proposes new mechanics or weapons, they must be explicit variants of existing Conker assets and respect the pickup-only philosophy.
