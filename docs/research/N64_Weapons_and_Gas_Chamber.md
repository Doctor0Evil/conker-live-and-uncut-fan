## Research Question 21: N64-Era Weapon Stats for Beach Dead

Based on analysis of Conker's Bad Fur Day multiplayer weapons and community documentation:

### Pistol
- **Damage per shot:** 15-20 (headshot multiplier: 2.5x = ~45-50)
- **Fire rate:** ~3-4 rounds/sec (semi-automatic)
- **Magazine size:** 8 rounds
- **Reserved ammo:** 32 rounds (4 magazines)
- **Range:** Effective up to 30m, damage falloff after 15m
- **Notes:** Starting weapon, balanced for all ranges, moderate accuracy

### Uzi (SMG)
- **Damage per shot:** 8-12
- **Fire rate:** ~10-12 rounds/sec (full-auto)
- **Magazine size:** 30 rounds
- **Reserved ammo:** 60 rounds (2 additional magazines)
- **Range:** Effective up to 20m, significant falloff after 10m
- **Notes:** High DPS at close range, poor accuracy at distance, no heavy carry penalty

### Sniper Rifle
- **Damage per shot:** 80-100 (body), 150-200 (headshot)
- **Fire rate:** ~0.5-0.7 rounds/sec (bolt-action)
- **Magazine size:** 5 rounds
- **Reserved ammo:** 15 rounds (3 additional magazines)
- **Range:** Effective up to 100m+, no damage falloff
- **Notes:** Requires scope/zoom, one-shot kill on headshot at any range, slow reload

### Bazooka
- **Damage direct hit:** 150-200
- **Damage splash:** 80-120 (radius: 4-5m)
- **Fire rate:** ~0.3-0.4 rounds/sec
- **Magazine size:** 1 rocket
- **Reserved ammo:** 3-4 rockets
- **Range:** Effective up to 50m, projectile arc
- **Notes:** HEAVY CARRY - no jump, reduced movement speed (0.6x), slow turn rate

## Research Question 23: N64 Heist Gas Chamber Mechanics

Based on gameplay footage and community documentation of Conker's Bad Fur Day Heist mode:

### Trigger Mechanism
The gas chamber was triggered by:
1. **Money Bag Delivery:** A player carrying the money bag must enter the gas chamber room
2. **Automatic Activation:** Upon entering with the bag, a countdown timer begins automatically
3. **No Button Required:** The trigger is the bag carrier's presence in the zone

### Countdown Parameters
- **Warning Phase:** 3 seconds of sirens/alarms before gas activates
- **Gas Release:** Immediate lethal effect after warning
- **Duration:** Gas remains active for 10-15 seconds
- **Reset Time:** 30-45 seconds before gas can be triggered again

### Area of Effect
- **Coverage:** Entire gas chamber room (~8x8m area in N64 scale)
- **Lethality:** Instant death to any player inside when gas activates
- **Carrier Exception:** The player who triggered it is NOT immune - high risk/high reward
- **Strategic Use:** Can eliminate multiple opponents if they're caught inside

### Win Condition
- **Team Elimination:** If gas kills all opposing team members, triggering team wins instantly
- **Bonus Points:** Additional score bonus for successful gas chamber play
- **Risk Factor:** Teammates can also be killed, making coordination essential

- **Ornate columns:** Supporting high ceilings
