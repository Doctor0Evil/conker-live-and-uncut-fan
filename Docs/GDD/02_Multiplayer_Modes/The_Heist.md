# Conker: Live & Uncut — The Heist Mode Design Document

## 1. Overview
"The Heist" is a 16-player, team-based competitive multiplayer mode pitting four teams of four players against one another in a fast-paced race to rob a bank. The mode was described in E3 2003 previews as one of the flagship multiplayer scenarios for Conker: Live & Uncut.

## 2. Core Objectives
- **Primary Goal:** Be the first team to successfully breach the bank vault, extract the cash, and reach the designated extraction point.
- **Secondary Objectives:** Sabotage other teams, eliminate rival players, and control key choke points to delay opponents.

## 3. Team Structure
- **Four teams, four players per team.**
- Teams are color-coded and identified by unique mascots (e.g., Squirrels, Tediz, Weasels, Uga Bugas).
- Each team spawns at a distinct "safehouse" located on opposite corners of the map.

## 4. Gameplay Phases
### Phase 1: Recon & Preparation
- Teams must navigate the city streets to reach the central bank building.
- Optional side objectives: hijack an armored car for extra cash, disable security cameras, or bribe NPCs for shortcuts.

### Phase 2: Breach
- Upon reaching the bank, teams must drill through the vault door.
- Drilling takes **60 seconds** and is interruptible if all team members in the vicinity are eliminated.
- Multiple teams can attempt to breach simultaneously; only the team that finishes drilling first gains access.

### Phase 3: Extraction
- Once the vault is open, cash bags spawn inside.
- Each player can carry **one cash bag** at a time (slows movement speed by 20%).
- Teams must transport cash bags back to their safehouse.
- **Win Condition:** First team to deposit **8 cash bags** at their safehouse wins.

## 5. Map Design
- **Setting:** A cartoonish, corrupt metropolis inspired by classic heist films.
- **Key Locations:**
  - Bank Vault (center)
  - Four team safehouses (corners)
  - Rooftop sniper nests
  - Underground sewer tunnels (alternate routes)
  - Police station (triggers NPC reinforcements if alarm is raised)

## 6. Special Mechanics
- **Alarm System:** Shooting inside the bank triggers an alarm, spawning hostile NPC cops that attack all players indiscriminately.
- **Cash Bag Drop:** When a player carrying cash is eliminated, the bag drops and can be picked up by any team.
- **Sabotage:** Teams can plant explosives on opposing safehouses, temporarily disabling cash deposit.

## 7. Customization Options (Planned for Uncut)
- Adjustable match length (cash bag target: 4/8/12)
- Friendly fire toggle
- Weapon restrictions (melee-only, pistols-only, etc.)
- Respawn time multiplier

## 8. Humor & Tone
- Spoofs of heist films (*Reservoir Dogs*, *Heat*, *Ocean's Eleven*).
- Conker's irreverent voice lines: "I'm here for the cash, not the conversation."
- Over-the-top cartoon violence: vault explosions, comically large money bags, and squeaky-voiced NPC hostages.
